The story behind SafeSpace

This platform was built by Antony — a concreter, businessman, father, and survivor based in Melbourne, Australia.

Over 25 years Antony built something real. Investment properties. A business. A family. A life that looked, from the outside, like it was working. Then in the space of a few years, almost all of it was gone — a marriage that ended, businesses that collapsed, savings wiped out by a partner who stole everything and fled the country.

What followed wasn't just financial devastation. It was the complete unravelling of identity. Who are you when everything you built is gone?

"The overthinking was the worst part. Not the loss itself — the loop. The mind using thinking to avoid feeling. I'd lie awake at 3am going over the same territory for the hundredth time, getting nowhere, exhausted but unable to stop."

Antony is currently completing a Certificate IV in Mental Health and building SafeSpace from scratch — with no funding, no investors, and no safety net.

Why peer support, not therapy

Therapy is valuable. But for many people in the place Antony has been, it isn't accessible. SafeSpace is built on a different premise. The most powerful thing you can hear when you're stuck isn't advice from a professional. It's someone saying: I've been there. Actually there. And I found a way through.

What SafeSpace is — and isn't

SafeSpace is peer support and mentorship. It is not a substitute for clinical mental health treatment. If you are in crisis, please contact Lifeline on 13 11 14 or Beyond Blue on 1300 22 4636.

Terms, Privacy & Disclaimer

Plain English. No legal jargon. Just honest.

What SafeSpace is

SafeSpace is a peer support and mentorship platform. It is not a clinical mental health service, not therapy, and not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact Lifeline on 13 11 14.

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, and password. As you use the platform we store your conversation history, mood check-ins, and profile data. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

How your data is protected

Your data is stored in Supabase — a secure, encrypted database. Your conversations are private to you. No human reads your conversations unless you explicitly request a 1-on-1 session.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all data at any time by contacting us. You can export your data on request.

Subscriptions and payments

The free plan is free forever. The 7-day trial gives you full access at no cost. After the trial, Pro membership is $19 AUD per month. Cancel anytime. Payments processed securely through Stripe.

Limitation of liability

SafeSpace is a peer support tool. We are not liable for decisions made based on conversations with the AI mentor. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services immediately.
